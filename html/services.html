What do we do?

At Innovative Circle Pvt. Ltd., we provide a wide array of multidisciplinary research consultancy services aimed at empowering individuals and organizations. Our core objectives include delivering professional research consultancy services that support informed decision-making through precise qualitative and quantitative data. We conduct empirical, policy-oriented research in socio-economic development, climate change, disaster risk management, business development, agriculture, health, and engineering. Additionally, we offer capacity-building training and technical assistance to governmental and non- governmental agencies, promoting social welfare by enhancing public awareness and facilitating knowledge exchange. Our commitment to quality assurance ensures that we maintain high standards throughout our engagements, fostering long-term relationships with our clients while delivering impactful research insights.

Services

Research and Analytics

The services offered include comprehensive support in Market Research and Data Analytics, helping clients gather and interpret vital data for informed decision-making.

Policy and Strategic Support

Policy Design Support and Analysis ensure robust frameworks tailored to specific needs, while Data Analytics further refines these insights.

Project and Capacity Development

Training and Capacity Building Workshops empower teams with essential skills, enhancing their ability to tackle challenges effectively.
