About Us

Innovative Circle Pvt. Ltd., established in 2013, is an independent and premier research consulting organization in Nepal. We are dedicated to addressing contemporary policy issues by bridging gaps between policy and practice. Our rigorous and scientific approach to policy and action research influences public and private policy and supports informed decision-making across various sectors.With a vision to become a Center of Excellence in quality consultancy, we offer a range of professional services tailored to meet the needs of our clients across multiple disciplines. Our mission is to create value for our clients through high-quality research and innovative solutions that drive socio-economic development. We specialize in providing data-driven insights to support policy formulation, project implementation, decision-making, and community development.Our experienced team leverages local expertise and scientific research methodologies to address social, economic, and environmental challenges.

We have an Advisory Panel, a Research Integrity Panel, full-time staff, Research/IT Interns from academia, and a significant number of thematic specialists/experts. The Advisory Board acts as the 'Board of Directors' providing overall guidance and strategic direction to the project.The Advisory Panel guides specific issues and contributes recommendations based on their availability and expertise. Acting as an 'Integrated Think Tank Team,' it provides technical and informal guidance. The Research Integrity Panel supports the research/project team in addressing subject matters and contributes to solutions as needed.The organogram of Innovative Circle Pvt. Ltd. illustrates its management framework integrating policy,managerial, and technical levels. Each project is supported by a dedicated ‘office team with consultants,’ as indicated in the organogram. The Advisory and Research Integrity Panel advise the project team for specific purposes based on their expertise
